your personal learning assistant

We learn and develop while we work, browse, read, watch, chat, game, like and share. We use a lot of tools and services.

But in case, we want to learn something new, how do we do it these days? Correct, we use Google, because the web is full of great content.

Unfortunately it takes a lot of time and effort.

Wouldn’t it be great, if there is that one tool, that supports your life integrated learning? Smart like magic, easy to use and perfectly talking to all your favorite apps and services?

Devoq is your essential assistant for learning.

It boosts all your learning management systems, collaboration tools and MOOC platforms. Or replaces them.

Stop clicking through expensive and wasting-time online courses. Stop using a bunch of internet resources to get the information you need.

Just enter a skill title, add some tags, upload content and connect the tools you love. That’s it, that’s all.
